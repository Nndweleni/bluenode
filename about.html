Why I Started Bluenode

I built Bluenode because I kept seeing small businesses either:

  1. Pay R10,000+ for websites they didn't need, or
  2. Struggle with DIY builders that never quite worked

Small businesses deserve professional websites at fair prices—without the corporate complexity or pushy sales tactics.

So I started Bluenode as a side project alongside my full-time work in tech. Lower overhead means better prices for you. Direct communication means faster decisions and no middlemen.

Our Philosophy: Boring Tech That Works

What We Believe

We believe in using proven, reliable technology rather than chasing the latest trends. Your website should be built on solid foundations that will still work five years from now.

"Boring tech" means HTML, CSS, JavaScript, WordPress, and other tools that have stood the test of time. No experimental frameworks. No unnecessary dependencies. No technical debt.

Your business deserves better than being a testing ground for new technology.

What We Don't Do

  • Over-engineer simple solutions
  • Lock you into proprietary systems
  • Pressure you with aggressive sales tactics
  • Promise unrealistic timelines or guarantees
  • Use your project to experiment with new tech
  • Hide costs or surprise you with fees

Our Core Values

What guides how we work and treat our clients.

Simplicity

Complex problems often have simple solutions. We focus on what actually matters: a clean design, fast performance, and content that serves your customers.

If a feature doesn't add real value, we won't suggest it.

Reliability

Your website needs to work when your customers need it. We build with proven technology, test thoroughly, and use stable hosting infrastructure.

Flashy is optional. Working is mandatory.

Transparency

No hidden fees. No vague timelines. No technical jargon designed to confuse. Our pricing is clear, our process is documented, and our terms are honest.

You'll always know what you're paying for and what to expect.

Accessibility

Web development shouldn't be out of reach for small businesses. We price our services to be affordable for individuals and small operations who need a professional online presence.

Quality doesn't have to be expensive.

What We Actually Sell

We're not selling software. We're not selling "digital transformation." We're selling outcomes.

When you work with Bluenode, you're buying:

  • A professional online presence - Look credible when customers search for you
  • Credibility - A real website builds trust with potential clients
  • Simplicity - No technical headaches or confusing dashboards
  • Peace of mind - Your site works, it's fast, and it's reliable
  • Time savings - We handle the technical work so you can focus on your business

Who We Work With

We're Built for Small Businesses

Bluenode is designed for individuals and small businesses who need a professional website without the complexity or cost of working with a traditional agency.

Ideal clients include:

  • Local service businesses (plumbers, electricians, consultants)
  • Freelancers and solopreneurs
  • Small shops and retailers
  • Professional practices (lawyers, accountants, therapists)
  • Startups and side projects
  • Anyone who needs a simple, working website
