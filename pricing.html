Delivery Timeline & Revision Policy

Delivery Timeline

Standard Basic and Standard websites: Delivered within up to 14 business days from the date all required content and access details are received.

Content submission requirement: Clients must provide all required content within 7 business days of project start. This includes:

  • Written text
  • Images and logos
  • Branding guidelines (if applicable)
  • Access credentials where required

Failure to provide content pauses the delivery timeline.

Revision Policy

Each website includes up to 3 revision rounds. Revisions are limited to:

  • Text edits
  • Layout adjustments
  • Minor design changes

Out of scope (additional fees apply):

  • Additional pages
  • New features or functionality
  • Integrations or plugins
  • Design changes beyond agreed layout

Out-of-scope work may incur additional fees and timeline extensions.

Important Note

Delivery timelines are estimates and not guaranteed. We are not liable for business losses caused by delays. See our Terms of Service for complete details.
