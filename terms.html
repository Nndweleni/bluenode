1. Scope of Services

We provide starter-level services including:

  • Website development

  • Email setup and configuration

Services are intended for individuals and small businesses only. No enterprise-grade guarantees are offered.

2. Website Development & Delivery

2.1 Delivery Timeline

Standard Basic and Standard websites are delivered within up to 14 business days from the date all required content and access details are received.

2.2 Content Submission Requirements

Clients must provide all required content within 7 business days of project start. Required content includes:

  • Written text
  • Images and logos
  • Branding guidelines (if applicable)
  • Access credentials where required

Failure to provide content pauses the delivery timeline.

2.3 Revisions

Each website includes up to 3 revision rounds. Revisions are limited to text edits, layout adjustments, and minor design changes. Additional revisions or major changes are billed separately.

2.4 Scope Control

The following are considered out of scope unless agreed in writing:

  • Additional pages

  • Design changes beyond agreed layout

Out-of-scope work may incur additional fees and timeline extensions.

2.5 Content Ownership

All client-supplied content remains the client's property. Content created by us becomes the client's property upon full payment. Clients confirm they have rights to all provided content.

2.6 No Guaranteed Deadlines

Delivery timelines are estimates and not guaranteed. We are not liable for business losses caused by delays.
